About

I moved to Nashville in 2009 to attend Belmont and study Music Business. After graduating in 2013, I realized that the music industry wasn't as fulfilling as I had hoped. In my spare time I began using Codecademy to learn the basics of web development. I realized that my passion was really in development. I knew I needed some education beyond what Codecademy could provide so I joined Nashville Software School in April 2016. It has been an amazing learning experience. The learning will never end as I continue on my journey as a web developer. I'm currently a Junior Instructor at Nashville Software School.

My dog, however, is still not happy with my choosen career path. Or really any career that involves leaving the house without him.
